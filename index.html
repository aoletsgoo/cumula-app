What makes cumula different

Most revision apps give every student the same path. cumula starts with the specific topics you haven't nailed yet — straight from your exam board's specification.

We map every GCSE spec point on AQA and Edexcel into the app. The diagnostic tells you which ones you're shaky on. The focus list tells you which to revise next. The activity grid keeps you honest about showing up.

For parents

Built for the way your teenager actually revises.

A private tutor's first session is almost always a diagnostic. cumula automates that part — and points your child at the topics that will move their grade the most, on the spec they're actually being examined on. No gimmicks, no streak-loss notifications, no in-app purchases.

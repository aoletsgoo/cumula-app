# cumula

Knowledge, compounded.

cumula tests what you already know, then builds a personal curriculum around the gaps. Modular. Measurable. Yours.

## Status

Pre-launch. Validating demand. Early access opens summer 2026.

## How it works

**01 — Tell us what to teach you**
Anything from Spanish to systems thinking. As broad or narrow as you want.

**02 — A short, smart diagnostic**
Eight to twelve questions calibrate your starting level. No guessing.

**03 — Your curriculum, generated**
Modules, sub-topics, theory and practice in balance. Tested as you go.

## What makes cumula different

Most learning apps assume you start at zero. cumula assumes you already know something — and builds around it.

Every curriculum is shaped by your real starting point, not a generic outline. The result: less time spent on what you've already mastered, more time spent on what genuinely moves you forward.

# cumula

Revision, accumulated.

cumula is a GCSE revision app built around your exam board's actual spec. It tests where you are, tells you which topics to focus on next, and tracks every day you show up.

## Status

Pre-launch. Validating demand. Early access opens summer 2026.

## Scope at launch

AQA and Edexcel · English Language, English Literature, and Maths · Year 10 and Year 11.

End goal: every UK exam board, every GCSE subject, every tier.

## How it works

**01 — Pick your board, subject, tier**
AQA or Edexcel. English Language, English Literature, or Maths. Foundation or Higher.

**02 — Take a quick diagnostic**
Eight to twelve questions mapped to your exam board's spec. cumula sees where you are, topic by topic.

**03 — Revise the right things, daily**
A focus list pulled straight from the spec. An activity grid that fills as you show up. Less guessing, more progress.

## What makes cumula different

Most revision apps give every student the same path. cumula starts with the specific topics *you* haven't nailed yet — straight from your exam board's specification.

We map every GCSE spec point on AQA and Edexcel into the app. The diagnostic tells you which ones you're shaky on. The focus list tells you which to revise next. The activity grid keeps you honest about showing up.
